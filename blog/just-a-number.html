Age is just a number

Who says you're too old to take up a new skill? Level Red Boxing proves that age is just a number and that you're strong at any level! Our dedicated team provides detailed and personalized coaching to allow you to reach your fitness goals. Not only do we provide the workout plans, we also provide the equipment and boxing gloves! From losing weight to building endurance, our team encourages you and keeps track of your progress unlike any other fitness group. We care about your experience and want to ensure you're comfortable and confident while you work out. After all, working out should be fun!

Our friendly, family-like environment will have you coming back for more. And before you know it, you'll be leveling up and achieving goals you never thought imaginable. Whether you're 16 or 85, who says you can't?! We say you can, and we'll prove it to you. Test out a class with us today and let us show you what you're capable of!
