Frequently Asked Questions

How early should I arrive before my first class?+-

First-timers are encouraged to arrive 30-15 minutes prior to class time.

What if I don't have boxing gloves?+-

Don't worry. We'll have gloves ready for you at your first visit.

I haven't worked out in a while. Are classes going to be ok for me?+-

Level Red is for everyBODY! Each class is set up for you to go at your own pace.

Will there be a trainer to help me out?+-

Absolutely! Your MOTIVATORS will be with you every step of the way.

Do I have to box people?+-

Absolutely not! Level Red Boxing is all about you vs. your bag.

Should I bring anything?+-

A workout towel and water are recommended.

What should I wear?+-

Wear athletic gear that helps you feel comfortable and confident.

Can I bring a friend?+-

Totally encouraged! Working out with a friend is a great way to enjoy your first class!
