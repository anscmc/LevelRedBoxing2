BRING GOALS

Everyone has a fitness goal. At Level Red Boxing, we want to help you focus only on just that and having fun. Don't bring a thing, we have complimentary gloves, protective hand wraps, towels and a heart rate monitor ready to go for your first class!

COACHES

Our motivators are 100% here for you! They're in your corner every step of the way and will be a huge part of your overall enjoyment of each class. Motivators at Level Red have been hand picked and have virtues that make them true master coaches. Your motivators are all super experienced, positive and knowledgeable.

VISUAL AIDS

Never miss a beat. Large monitors displaying current exercises and rep count are in ever corner so you'll never be confused. With visual and live coaching aids all over the room, you'll always know what exercise you're on, how to do it and how much.

YOU VS. THE BAG

Don't worry, at Level Red, we never box with each other. This is about you and the bag. Your goals, motivations and victories.

MEASUREMENTS

Body tracking is a complimentary and non-mandatory service. Get a total body analytic work up that will help you see where you are and where you should be aiming for. In just 5 minutes, you'll know your circumference measurements, body fat % and lean mass %.


Important Note:

If you're taking your first class, please arrive 10-20 minutes early so we can get you all set up. We can't wait to see you here!
